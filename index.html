Constraints

Why does software development slow down over time? One of the many reasons is that the existing code places constraints on your ability to go forward. You must keep all prior features working while adding the next.

This interactive page gives you a simple activity that demonstrates why this happens.

Your Goal

Fill the grid above with the numbers 1 through 9 such that:

  • The right side sums to
  • The top sums to
  • The diagonal from bottom right to top left sums to

Controls

To fill in the grid, just type the numbers on your keyboard. You can also move the selection around using the enter or arrow keys.
